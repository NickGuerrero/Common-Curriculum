Final Defense Presentation

Sprint 4 · Due , noon · 18 pts

Defense outline (3 minutes)

Format: 3 min presentation + 2 min Q&A · W15 or W16 (your choice).

1
30 sec · The belief that changed. One thing you believe about your ability to learn that has changed since the semester started. State the before and the after clearly.
2
45 sec · The moment. The specific point — which assignment, which conversation, which moment of stuck — where you realized the shift. Not “throughout the semester.” A moment.
3
75 sec · The evidence. Concrete evidence the shift is real. Your Sprint 4 gap-closing work is the freshest example. Earlier sprints, peer conversations, reflections all count.
4
30 sec · Going forward. How this changes your work from here — next semester, first job, next gap you’ll hit.

Anticipate in Q&A

  • What’s the strongest evidence this shift is real and not just a story you’re telling?
  • Where in the semester did you NOT demonstrate this change? What does that tell you?
  • If you hit a similar learning gap in 6 months, what would you do differently?
  • What’s one thing about your ability to learn that has NOT changed, that you thought would?
