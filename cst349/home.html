Becoming a Self-Directed Learner

Technical skills get you hired. Self-directed learning keeps you relevant.
Over the semester you'll run the same cycle again and again — spot a skill gap, develop it with real accountability, and prove your growth — until directing your own learning becomes a habit you keep long after this course.
Computing Talent
Initiative

A two-week orientation eases you in. Then three skill-building sprints, with support that tapers as your independence grows. A two-week wrap-up to prove it.
