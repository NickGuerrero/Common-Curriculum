Dojo Lab: Synthesis Gap Audit

Learning goal

Apply the core idea from Dojo Lab: Synthesis Gap Audit in a focused Canvas submission.

Purpose

Use AI to audit your portfolio for evidence, gaps, repetition, and visible human judgment before you draft the final report.

Pre-Dojo Pause

Before using AI, make your own list of the strongest evidence you have and the weakest part of your final argument.

Open The Dojo

Use the Open the Symbiotic Thinking Dojo link for this lab. First-time users should open Learn About -> Privacy & Setup and configure a Gemini API key in the browser before starting.

Do not paste confidential workplace, client, education, patient, or personal data into the Dojo. De-identify details and keep sensitive information out of the prompt.

Dojo Prompt

Use this prompt with the Dojo:

I am preparing my final Integrated Problem Document and Readiness Report.

Here are the parts I have:
- Original goal:
- Reframed problem:
- 3Cs log:
- Stakeholder validation:
- Learning journey:
- Current problem statement:

Use Learn + Solve + Build mode. Start with @connector. Help me find:
1. What belongs in the final argument?
2. What repeats or distracts?
3. What gaps would a reader notice?
4. What evidence is strongest?
5. What evidence is weakest?

Then use @reflector. Check whether my human judgment is visible:
- Where did I decide?
- Where did I revise based on evidence?
- Where did I push back on AI?
- Where did stakeholder context matter?

Then use @auditor. Check whether the final document direction is coherent, evidence-backed, and more than a compilation of earlier artifacts.

Judgment Step

Do not let the Dojo decide readiness for you. Use it to identify gaps and organization choices. You make the final judgment.

Deliverable

Submit a text entry with:

  • Synthesis outline.
  • Gap list.
  • Evidence list.
  • Human judgment list.
  • One paragraph explaining how the final document will be more than a compilation.

Portfolio Capture

Save this audit as your planning evidence for the final report.
