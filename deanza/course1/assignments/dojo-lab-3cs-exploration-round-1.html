Dojo Lab: 3Cs Exploration Round 1

Learning goal

Apply the core idea from Dojo Lab: 3Cs Exploration Round 1 in a focused Canvas submission.

Purpose

Use the Dojo to explore your reframed problem through Context, Choices, and Confirmation.

Pre-Dojo Pause

Before using AI, write three context details you believe matter and one thing you do not want AI to assume.

Dojo Prompt

Use this prompt with the Dojo:

I am working on this reframed problem:
[paste problem]

Act as Framer. Help me explore the problem using the 3Cs.

Context:
- What context would you need to understand this problem better?
- What details should I provide because they are specific to my situation?
- What constraints might matter?

Choices:
- What are different ways to frame or approach this problem?
- What tradeoffs come with each approach?
- What assumptions does each approach depend on?

Confirmation:
- What would need to be verified before I trust any direction?
- What evidence could come from stakeholders, data, observation, or small tests?

Pushback Step

Ask the Dojo to revise at least one choice after you add more context or challenge an assumption.

Deliverable

Submit a text entry with a 3-column log:

  • Context added.
  • Choices surfaced.
  • Confirmation needed.

Also include the pushback you gave AI and what changed after that pushback.
