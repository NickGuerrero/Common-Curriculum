Readiness Self-Check

Purpose

Check whether your final readiness judgment is evidence-backed and readable. This is an applied AI-guided check on synthesis, stakeholder evidence, human contribution, and readiness with conditions.

How It Works

  1. Write your current readiness judgment.
  2. Generate AI follow-up questions.
  3. Revise your answer so it connects evidence, conditions, stakeholder grounding, and your human contribution.
  4. Use the deeper AI guidance button if the judgment sounds polished but unsupported.
  5. Save the refined response in the activity.

What To Submit

Download the JSON response file from the activity and upload it to Canvas. Your final response should help you decide what must still change before the final integrated submission.
