Sprint 4 Reflection: What I Learned And How I Know

Learning goal

Apply the core idea from Sprint 4 Reflection: What I Learned And How I Know in a focused Canvas submission.

Purpose

Reflect on how your learning changed the problem and what you are still not ready to claim.

Task Instructions

  1. Review your learning path, sources, AI prompts, and verification notes.
  2. Write about enough learning for this stage, not complete mastery.
  3. Separate what you know, what you checked, and what remains uncertain.

Deliverable

Submit a text entry with:

  • What you learned.
  • How you know you learned it well enough for this course stage.
  • What AI explained well.
  • What you had to verify elsewhere.
  • What you are still not ready to claim.

Submission Format

Text entry in Canvas.

Evidence Of Success

Your submission should be specific enough that another person can understand your context, your reasoning, and what you are ready to do next. Do not submit a polished AI answer without showing your own judgment.

Portfolio Capture

Save this reflection as evidence for your final readiness judgment.
