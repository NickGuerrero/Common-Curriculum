Final Integrated Problem Document and Readiness Report

Learning goal

Apply the core idea from Final Integrated Problem Document and Readiness Report in a focused Canvas submission.

Purpose

Submit the culminating problem document for the course. The report should show what problem you are ready to carry forward, what evidence supports that readiness, and why your human judgment matters.

Task Instructions

  1. Revise your draft using the synthesis audit and peer review.
  2. Write for an outside reader who needs to understand the problem and your readiness without seeing every prior assignment.
  3. Choose one readiness judgment: ready to carry forward, ready with conditions, or not ready yet with a clear next step.
  4. Name what AI contributed and what you contributed that AI alone could not.

Deliverable

Submit a text entry with:

  • Executive summary: the problem and why it matters.
  • Final problem statement.
  • Evolution of the problem: from original goal to current frame.
  • Stakeholder synthesis: needs, constraints, tensions, and validation evidence.
  • 3Cs synthesis: context, choices, and confirmation.
  • Self-directed learning synthesis: gap, learning path, what changed, what remains uncertain.
  • Problem-solving goals and success criteria.
  • Human and AI contribution split.
  • Readiness judgment: ready to carry forward, ready with conditions, or not ready yet with a clear reason and next step.
  • Next move and final Goal Plan check.

Submission Format

Text entry in Canvas.

Evidence Of Success

Your submission should be specific enough that another person can understand your context, your reasoning, and what you are ready to do next. Do not submit a polished AI answer without showing your own judgment.
