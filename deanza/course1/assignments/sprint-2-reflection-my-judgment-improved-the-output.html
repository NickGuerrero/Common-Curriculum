Sprint 2 Reflection: My Judgment Improved The Output

Purpose

Reflect on how your direction, critique, and judgment changed the AI interaction.

Task Instructions

  1. Review your first prompt, later prompt, critique, and selected direction.
  2. Write an initial reflection in the activity about one moment where your judgment changed the output.
  3. Generate AI follow-up questions and use them to identify what is still vague, generic, or unsupported.
  4. Revise your reflection so it names what still needs confirmation through real evidence.
  5. Use the deeper AI guidance button if your answer does not yet show your contribution clearly.
  6. Save the refined response in the activity.

Deliverable

Your refined reflection should answer:

  • Where you took the lead.
  • What AI made easier.
  • What AI made riskier.
  • Which choice you are carrying forward.
  • Why that choice is defensible right now.

Submission Format

Download the JSON response file from the activity and upload it to Canvas.

Evidence Of Success

Your final response should be specific enough that another person can understand your context, your reasoning, and what you are ready to do next. Do not submit a polished AI answer without showing your own judgment.

Portfolio Capture

Save this reflection with your AI Exploration Log. It will help you show your human contribution in the final readiness report.
