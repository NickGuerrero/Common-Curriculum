Sprint 2 Reflection: My Judgment Improved The Output

Learning goal

Apply the core idea from Sprint 2 Reflection: My Judgment Improved The Output in a focused Canvas submission.

Purpose

Reflect on how your direction, critique, and judgment changed the AI interaction.

Task Instructions

  1. Review your first prompt, later prompt, critique, and selected direction.
  2. Write about one moment where your judgment changed the output.
  3. Name what still needs confirmation through real evidence.

Deliverable

Submit a text entry with:

  • Where you took the lead.
  • What AI made easier.
  • What AI made riskier.
  • Which choice you are carrying forward.
  • Why that choice is defensible right now.

Submission Format

Text entry in Canvas.

Evidence Of Success

Your submission should be specific enough that another person can understand your context, your reasoning, and what you are ready to do next. Do not submit a polished AI answer without showing your own judgment.

Portfolio Capture

Save this reflection with your AI Exploration Log. It will help you show your human contribution in the final readiness report.
