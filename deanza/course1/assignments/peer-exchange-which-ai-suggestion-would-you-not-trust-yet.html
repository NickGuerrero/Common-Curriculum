Peer Exchange: Which AI Suggestion Would You Not Trust Yet?

Purpose

Use AI to sharpen your skepticism. The goal is not to reject AI. The goal is to separate useful possibilities from claims that still require confirmation.

How It Works

  1. Write about one plausible AI suggestion you do not trust yet.
  2. Generate AI follow-up questions.
  3. Use the questions to identify hidden assumptions, missing evidence, and stakeholder perspectives.
  4. Use the deeper AI guidance button for another round if your confirmation plan is still vague.
  5. Save the refined response in the activity.

What To Submit

Download the JSON response file from the activity and upload it to Canvas. Your final response should make clear what you will not use yet, why, and what would make it responsible to use later.
