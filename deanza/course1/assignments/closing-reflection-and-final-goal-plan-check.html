Closing Reflection and Final Goal Plan Check

Learning goal

Apply the core idea from Closing Reflection and Final Goal Plan Check in a focused Canvas submission.

Purpose

Close the course by naming what changed in how you frame problems, how AI contributed, and what your next responsible move is.

Task Instructions

  1. Review your Week 1 goal, final report, stakeholder evidence, learning journey, and readiness claim.
  2. Write honestly about change over time. Do not make the course sound cleaner than it was.
  3. End with the next move you can actually take.

Deliverable

Submit a text entry with:

  • What changed most in how you frame problems.
  • What role AI played across the course.
  • Where your own judgment mattered most.
  • What would have been worse if you had handed this problem to AI.
  • Your next move.

Submission Format

Text entry in Canvas.

Evidence Of Success

Your submission should be specific enough that another person can understand your context, your reasoning, and what you are ready to do next. Do not submit a polished AI answer without showing your own judgment.

Portfolio Capture

Save this reflection with the final report. It shows the metacognitive arc of the course.
