Sprint 3 Reflection and Mid-Course Goal Plan Revision

Learning goal

Apply the core idea from Sprint 3 Reflection and Mid-Course Goal Plan Revision in a focused Canvas submission.

Purpose

Pause at mid-course to name what stakeholder evidence changed and how your Goal Plan should adapt.

Task Instructions

  1. Review your stakeholder map, validation notes, and revised problem statement.
  2. Compare what AI anticipated with what a real person actually helped you see.
  3. Revise the Goal Plan for the rest of the course.

Deliverable

Submit a text entry with:

  • What a real person helped you see that AI did not.
  • What part of your frame changed.
  • What tension or conflict you are carrying forward.
  • Your revised Goal Plan for the rest of the course.

Submission Format

Text entry in Canvas.

Evidence Of Success
