Goal Plan and Problem Reframing Document

Learning goal

Apply the core idea from Goal Plan and Problem Reframing Document in a focused Canvas submission.

Purpose

Create the first durable course artifact. This document distinguishes your goal from the problem you will test and refine over the rest of the course.

Task Instructions

  1. Review your Pre-Dojo Goal Pause, Dojo notes, and peer exchange responses.
  2. Revise your problem statement so it is specific, real, current, and workable in the time available.
  3. Name what AI helped you see and what you decided not to use.
  4. Write a Goal Plan that explains how you will keep moving the problem forward during the course.

Deliverable

Submit a text entry with:

  • Goal statement.
  • Why this goal matters now.
  • Original surface request or first problem statement.
  • Abstraction ladder analysis.
  • Reframed problem statement.
  • Scope for the next eight weeks.
  • People affected.
  • Evidence needed next.
  • Goal Plan: how you will move this forward during the course.

Submission Format

Text entry in Canvas.

Evidence Of Success

Your submission should be specific enough that another person can understand your context, your reasoning, and what you are ready to do next. Do not submit a polished AI answer without showing your own judgment.

Portfolio Capture

This document becomes the starting point for Sprint 2. Keep a copy where you can revise it later instead of treating it as finished forever.
