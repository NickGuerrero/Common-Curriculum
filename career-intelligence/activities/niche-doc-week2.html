Niche Doc — Week 2 Update

Learning goal

Sharpen your niche into 2–3 role types so you know exactly which jobs to chase.

Your Niche Doc is a living document. This week, add a new iteration that reflects what you have learned — and aim to finish it by the end of the session.

What to do

  1. Open your Niche Doc (your own copy from Week 1).
  2. Fold in what surfaced this week: the Resume Story Swap, your 10 analyzed job postings, and your sharpened role-type hypotheses.
  3. Sharpen your niche so it is more specific than last week — across what you bring, what the market wants, and where they match.

Name your 2–3 role types

Before you update your Niche Doc, work these through with an AI sparring-partner:

  1. Name your 2–3 role types in plain words.
  2. For each, the 3–5 phrases or requirements that showed up again and again across your 10 postings.
  3. Which role type has the most evidence in your background? Which is more aspiration than evidence right now?
  4. What’s one thing still missing before this niche is fully backed by evidence?
Name them like this

Too broad: “software engineering jobs.”

Specific: “backend / data-platform engineer at mid-size product companies — APIs and pipelines over large datasets.” Specific enough that you know which postings to chase.
