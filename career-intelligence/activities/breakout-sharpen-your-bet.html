Career Intelligence · Week 4 · Begin Own Your Search

Breakout — Sharpen Your Bet

Learning goal

Leave the session with a bet a partner couldn’t poke a hole in, pressure-tested first by a peer and then by your AI Dojo.

Two short rounds, in pairs. First a human read, then your AI Dojo as the second mirror. You’ll capture the result here and carry it straight into your Search Log.

In the breakout

Round 1 · Peer conversation

Pressure-test each other’s bet

  1. Say your bet out loud: a geography bet (B = a region you have ties to) or a sector bet (B = an industry where your background is a real edge). Name your A (default) and B (your bet), and why B is a real reason to win.
  2. Your partner pushes back: Is B specific? Could I tell your A group from your B group? Is your reason to win B a genuine edge, not just “somewhere less crowded”?
  3. Name the signal you’ll watch, and what would make you adjust. Then swap.
Round 2 · Peer + AI

Run it through your Search Strategy Dojo

  1. Add the Search Strategy module to your Dojo (Dojo library).
  2. Talk through your bet; let it push on your fair-test setup (one resume for both groups, your B-edge visible) and your signal.
  3. At the end, have it produce its summary block — copy it for your submission and your Search Log.
Peer first, AI second — your call throughout. The partner catches what an AI misses; the Dojo is the second mirror, not the decider. You make every call about what to claim and what to change.

Capture & submit

Submission type: Text entry. Paste in:

  • Your bet: A (your default), B (your bet — geography or sector), and why B is your edge.
  • Two pushes your partner gave you — and what you changed because of them.
  • Your Search Strategy Dojo summary block (paste it).
