Career Intelligence · Week 4 · Begin Own Your Search

Breakout — Sharpen Your Bet

Learning goal

Two rounds: first reflect on the week’s shift out loud with a partner (behavioral interview practice), then sharpen your actual bet with your AI Dojo.

Two short rounds, in pairs. First you reflect on the shift out loud (interview practice), then your AI Dojo helps you sharpen your bet. Capture both here and carry your bet into your Search Log.

In the breakout

Round 1 · Peer conversation · behavioral interview practice

Reflect on the shift, out loud

In pairs, take turns: one of you talks it through while the other listens and asks follow-ups, like a behavioral interview. Then swap.

  1. In your own words, what are the mindset shift and the workflow shift this week?
  2. Do you believe the payoff — that searching to learn puts you in the path of good opportunities? Why, or why not?
  3. How will you adapt this into your own job search?

The skill you’re practicing: saying your thinking clearly, out loud, the way an interview demands.

Round 2 · Peer + AI

Sharpen your bet with the Dojo

  1. Add the Search Strategy module to your Dojo (Dojo library).
  2. Name your bet — geography or sector, your A (default) and B (your bet) — and let the Dojo push on your fair-test setup (one resume for both groups, your B-edge visible) and your signal.
  3. At the end, have it produce its summary block — copy it for your submission and your Search Log.
Peer first, AI second — your call throughout. The partner catches what an AI misses; the Dojo is the second mirror, not the decider. You make every call about what to claim and what to change.

Capture & submit

Submission type: Text entry. Paste in:

  • The shift, in your words: the mindset and workflow shifts, whether you believe the payoff, and how you’ll adapt it into your search.
  • Your bet: A (your default), B (your bet — geography or sector), and why B is your edge.
  • Your Search Strategy Dojo summary block (paste it).
