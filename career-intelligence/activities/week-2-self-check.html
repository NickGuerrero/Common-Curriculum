Week 2 Self-Check

Learning goal

Make sure your niche is specific and backed by real postings before you build your search on it.

Three questions to reflect on. Write a few honest sentences for each — this is for your own thinking, not for a grade. There are no wrong answers; not sure yet is a fine place to be, as long as you say why.

  1. Where are you now? Name the one to three specific role types you are considering after this week — specific enough to act on, not just a job in tech. What in your own background points you toward them?
  2. What did the market show you? Across the job postings you looked at this week, what skills, words, or requirements kept coming up — and where does your current niche match them, or miss them?
  3. What is still rough? Where does your niche still feel too broad, too narrow, or like a guess rather than evidence-backed — and what is one thing you will do next to sharpen it?
Do question 1 by hand. Write your role types out on paper, then photograph it — writing by hand makes you commit to specifics instead of hedging. Type your answers to questions 2 and 3.
