Career Intelligence

Your six-week journey

From "I'm not sure what I'm aiming at" to a sharp niche, a resume that proves it, and a job search you run like an experiment. Here's the whole path — and what you walk away with at each step.

Know Your Niche

Look honestly at what you uniquely bring and where the market actually needs it.

You leave withA first niche — a direction worth exploring, in your own words.

Sharpen Your Niche

Pull 10 real job postings, sort them into 2–3 role types, and rebuild your resume to match.

You leave with10 real targets and a resume that points straight at them.

Resume Workshop + Your Dojo

Get live feedback on your resume, defend your niche with evidence, and set up an AI Dojo you'll keep using.

You leave withA recruiter-ready resume and your own AI coach for the whole search.

Begin Own Your Search

Turn your niche into a testable plan — your Search Strategy Hypothesis: where you're aiming, why you fit, and how you'll test it.

You leave withA clear, testable search plan you can actually run.

Iteration 1 + Fireside

Run your first real search cycle — make actual moves — and hear from university recruiters how hiring really works.

You leave withReal moves made, and insider insight into the process.

Iteration 2 + Networking

Adjust your search on what you learned, meet engineers at the networking event, and see how far your thinking has come.

You leave withA sharper search and real industry connections.

Show your work — earn your mentor

Finish strong: book a 1-on-1 with the team to walk us through what you've built — your niche, your resume, your search. Complete the program and clear that bar, and you earn a code to connect with a MentorCruise mentor in your field — we cover your first outreach. It's your first real step from the program into the industry.
