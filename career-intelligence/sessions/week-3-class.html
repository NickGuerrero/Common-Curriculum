Week 3 · Live session

Resume Review Workshop

Where Week 2 (your resume and niche) becomes a resume that proves your fit — and gets you ready to begin Own Your Search in Week 4.

What you should have

Bring these so the workshop has something real to work on:

  • Your current resume — the Resume v2 you built in Week 2.
  • One real job posting you’re targeting — ideally one you’ve already tailored your resume toward.
  • Your Niche Doc — your 2–3 role types and the evidence behind them.

Session plan

Designed by Jeff — a scaffold, not a script.

Session plan — to be added

Run the session however works best: open with a live critique of a volunteer’s resume, walk through one strong example project, demo the Resume Dojo, or your own approach. The only target is the three questions below — design the time so students can answer them by the end.

Need a starting point? One possible shape
Open — frame the bridge: the resume now has to prove the niche. Optionally model the lens with one live critique.
Work — students apply the same lens to their own resume (your critique, a peer, or the Resume Dojo) and check their niche against the evidence.
Close — each student names their top resume change. Point them to setting up the Resume Dojo and the take-home review.

The target — students thinking for themselves about their resume

By the end, a student should be able to reason about their own resume — judge what’s working, what’s weak, and what to change — without being told. The three questions below are examples of what that sounds like; use them, or pick three of your own that get at the same independent thinking.

  1. If a recruiter reads only the top third of your resume for ten seconds, what specifically tells them you fit this role — and what’s missing?
  2. What is the strongest piece of evidence on your resume for the role you’re targeting, and which claim is weakest or hardest to back up?
  3. Which one bullet will you rewrite first, and what specific result or number will you add to make it stronger?

After the session, students set up their Resume Dojo and run the guided review on their own, then complete the Week 3 self-check.
